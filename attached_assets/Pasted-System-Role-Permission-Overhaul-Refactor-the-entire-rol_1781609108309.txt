System Role & Permission Overhaul
Refactor the entire role and permission system for the department app. Below are the exact rules for each role.

GENERAL ARCHITECTURE

On every login (and app foreground resume), fetch the user's full role profile from the backend, including a is_course_rep flag and course_rep_for (course ID).
Never hardcode role UI — always derive it from the fetched role profile.
All data queries must be scoped server-side, not just hidden in the UI.


ADMIN ROLE
Admin is a system operator only. They do not interact with individual students directly.
Admin can:

Approve, reject, and suspend user accounts
Create and manage courses (name, code, semester, assign lecturer)
Set contribution dues and view payment records across the department
View department-wide analytics (attendance rates, payment completion, active users)
Post announcements to the whole department
Configure department settings (semester, session, branding)
Export reports as PDF or CSV

Admin cannot:

Assign course reps
Message individual students
Add students to specific courses
View or manage class sessions directly


LECTURER ROLE
Lecturer only sees and manages courses they are explicitly assigned to. All queries must filter by lecturer_id.
Lecturer can:

View only their assigned courses
View only students enrolled in their assigned courses
Create and manage class sessions for their courses only
Generate and close attendance QR codes for their classes
View attendance records and stats for their classes and students
Assign or remove a course rep from their course roster
Post announcements scoped to their course only
Upload materials (notes, slides) per class session
Message their course rep directly
Flag students with low attendance

Lecturer cannot:

See courses assigned to other lecturers
See students not enrolled in their courses
Access contribution or payment data
Access department-wide analytics or settings


COURSE REP ROLE
Course rep is not a separate account. It is a flag on a student account (is_course_rep: true, course_rep_for: courseId). It is assigned and removed by the lecturer of that course.
When is_course_rep is true, the student gets all normal student features plus:

A visible rep badge on their profile
Ability to post announcements to their course
View the full classmates list (name + contact) for their course
Direct message their lecturer
Raise a course issue (submitted to the lecturer)
View contribution payment status for their course (names + paid/unpaid only, no amounts)
View course attendance overview as aggregate stats (no individual breakdown)

When the rep flag is removed, all extra features disappear on next role refresh. No separate account deletion needed.

STUDENT ROLE
Student can:

View their timetable and today's classes
Scan QR code to mark attendance for live classes
View their own attendance stats broken down by course
Read announcements from admin, their lecturer, and their course rep
View and pay their contributions
Download class materials uploaded by their lecturer
Edit their own profile
Submit an issue through their course rep

Student cannot:

See other students' attendance or profiles
Access any lecturer or admin features
Post announcements


COURSE REP ASSIGNMENT FLOW

Only the lecturer of a course can assign or remove a course rep.
The lecturer picks from the enrolled student roster of that specific course.
Assignment immediately updates the student's is_course_rep flag and course_rep_for field in the database.
The student receives an in-app notification that they have been made course rep.
If the student is removed as rep, they receive a notification and the flag is cleared.
Only one active course rep per course at a time. Assigning a new one automatically removes the previous.


ROLE CHECK ON LOGIN
On login and every time the app resumes from background:

Fetch the user's role profile from the backend.
If role is student, also check is_course_rep and course_rep_for.
Render the dashboard and navigation based on the fetched role, not a cached value.
If is_course_rep changed since last session, show the appropriate notification banner.
